Whats happening at Vizag DEVFEST 2019?

Expert Talks

Indulge in sessions where experts from all around the country share tips, techniques and learnings on the latest technologies, coolest frameworks and smartest apps.

Live Sessions

Unleash the developer within you with sessions that enable to quickly build an app, design a website or deploy some code. Embrace a world of possibilities.

Inspirational Stories

Get amazed and motivated by the success stories of tech entrepreneurs and professionals who have stood their ground and succeeded in their moonshots.

Networking Fun

Experience having fun while learning. Network and share with co-attendees who have had a different path than yours, all while having fun together.
